01 : About me

Introduction

Hello, I'm Gargi Mittal, a computer science graduate with a strong foundation in Python and web development. My journey also spans Android and Java development, and I’ve recently been exploring artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Journey

In my 11th and 12th standard, I began my journey with Python, developing a bill management system as my final school project. Driven by a passion for building things from scratch, I chose to pursue engineering and recently graduated with a B.Tech in Computer Science. Throughout college, I served as the Technical Head, where I led a team to create and maintain my college’s first-ever fest website, making it fully responsive and designing the entire event page. Along the way, I participated in two hackathons, completed internships in frontend and WordPress development, and worked on projects like an ASL translator hub and a herbal plant recommender. These experiences not only sharpened my technical skills but also taught me the value of collaboration, leadership, and creating solutions that truly make an impact.
